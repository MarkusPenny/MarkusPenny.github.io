Ramen

Ramen is a really simple dish that doesn't take more than a few minutes to make. It tneds to be quite salty due to the soy sauce, however when done properly, its high sodium level is very much worth it.



  • Serves: 2
  • Preperation time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking time: 10 minutes


You will need:

  • 1 medium saucepan
  • 1 small saucepan (Optional)
  • 1 frying pan
  • A mixing spoon
  • 1 sharp knife
  • 2 bowls


Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Water 400ml
Egg/Rice noodles 1 handful/pre-portioned ball
Raw chicken 1 breast
Spring onion 3-4 stalks
Peas 1/2 a cup
Sweetcorn 1/2 a cup
Garlic 1 clove
Soy sauce 4 tbs
Ginger powder 2 tbsps
Pepper A pinch
Optional ingredients Quantity
Raw beef Equal to 1 chicken breast
Boiled egg, halved or quartered 2 eggs
Carrot, chopped very thin 1/2 a carrot
Chilli, dried 1 pinch

Method

Step 1
Prepare your meats and vegetables; cut the chicken and (if you choose to add it) beef into thin slices; slice the spring onions into small chunks, and if you choose to add carrot, use a peeler to slice it so thin it's translucent
Step 2
Put the water in the saucepan and place over a medium heat. Fry the meat in the frying pan with a little bit of oil to stop it sticking. Continue frying until there are no raw spots.

If you choose to have boiled eggs in your ramen, add 2 cups of water to a small saucepan and put it over a high heat.
Step 3
Transfer the meats to a plate.
